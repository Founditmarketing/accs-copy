Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our in-home care services, eligibility, costs, and waiver programs.

Getting Started

What services does ACCS provide?

ACCS provides comprehensive in-home care services including personal care assistance, companionship, meal preparation, grocery shopping, light housekeeping, transportation, 24-hour care, and specialized Alzheimer's/dementia care. Our goal is to help individuals maintain independence while remaining in the comfort of their home.

Who qualifies for in-home care services?

Our services are available to elderly, disabled, and recovering adults who need assistance with daily living activities. Eligibility varies by funding source — Medicaid waiver recipients, Veterans through the VA Community Care program, and anyone who wishes to pay privately can all receive care through ACCS.

How do I get started with ACCS?

Getting started is simple. Call our Ball office at 318-640-7422 or our Leesville office at 337-404-9292. You can also visit our contact page to submit a request online. A supervisor will be assigned to assess your needs and match you with the right caregiver.

What areas do you serve?

We serve communities across Central and Western Louisiana from our two offices — Ball (near Alexandria) and Leesville. Our service area covers multiple parishes. Contact us to confirm availability in your specific area.

Insurance & Payment

Does ACCS accept Medicaid?

Yes. We are an approved provider for multiple Louisiana Medicaid waiver programs including NOW (New Opportunities Waiver), ROW (Residential Options Waiver), SIL (Supports for Independent Living), CCW (Community Choices Waiver), LTPCS (Long-Term Personal Care Services), EPSDT (Early Periodic Screening, Diagnosis & Treatment), and Children's Choice.

Do you accept VA Community Care?

Yes. We are proud to serve our Veterans through the VA Community Care program. If you are a Veteran who has been approved for in-home care through the VA, contact us to get matched with a dedicated caregiver.

What if I don't have Medicaid or VA coverage?

We offer private pay options for individuals who wish to receive care without going through a waiver program. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your care needs and schedule.

Is there a cost for an initial assessment?

No. We provide a free initial consultation and needs assessment to determine what level of care would best serve you or your loved one.

Care & Caregivers

Are your caregivers trained and background-checked?

Absolutely. Every ACCS caregiver undergoes thorough background screening and receives comprehensive training before being placed with a client. We maintain ongoing training programs to ensure our team provides the highest standard of care.

Can I choose my caregiver?

We do our best to match you with a caregiver who fits your personality, needs, and preferences. If at any time you'd like to request a different caregiver, simply speak with your supervisor and we will accommodate.

What happens if my regular caregiver is unavailable?

If your regular caregiver is unavailable due to illness or personal reasons, your supervisor will arrange a qualified substitute to ensure continuity of care. We never leave our clients without support.

Is care available 24/7?

Yes. ACCS provides care 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays. Whether you need a few hours of daily assistance or round-the-clock care, we build a schedule that works for you.

Employment

How do I apply to work at ACCS?

Visit our careers page to fill out an application online. We hire Direct Service Workers (DSWs), supervisors, and office staff. We're always looking for compassionate people to join our team.

Do I need prior experience in caregiving?

While prior experience is valued, it is not always required. We provide comprehensive training for all new hires. What matters most is your compassion, reliability, and genuine desire to help others.
