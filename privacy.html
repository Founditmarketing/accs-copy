Introduction

Alternative Concept Care Services ("ACCS," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients, their families, our employees, and visitors to our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out a contact form on our website
  • Submit an employment application
  • Request information about our services
  • Engage with us by phone or email

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and any other information you choose to provide.

Health Information

In the course of providing care services, we may collect protected health information (PHI) as needed to deliver appropriate care. All health information is handled in compliance with applicable federal and state laws, including HIPAA where applicable.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages viewed. This information is used solely for site analytics and performance improvement.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • Process employment applications
  • Coordinate and deliver in-home care services
  • Communicate with clients and their families about care
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service partners: Healthcare providers, insurance companies, and Medicaid/VA programs as necessary to coordinate your care
  • Legal compliance: When required by law, court order, or governmental regulation
  • Business operations: With trusted service providers who assist in operating our website and conducting our business, subject to confidentiality agreements

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at alternative@accs6.com or call 318-640-7422.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can set your browser to refuse all or some cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If you disable cookies, some parts of the site may not function properly.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.
